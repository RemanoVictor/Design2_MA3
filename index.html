Design System of HomeFair.no

Brand Personality

The brand personality of home.no is one that wnats to create ease and efficiency in the real estate market. It looks to take away the stress of finding the right realtor whi is able to get you the best price when buying or selling a homestead. This is hopefully achieved by using AI which removes the risk of human error.

Target Audience

The target audience for Home.no would be inviduals who are looking to either buy or sell their apartment or house. Understandably it may seem that it appeals more to the younger generation as this relies heavily on technology, but the process is almost seemless and extremely easy to get used to.

Typography:

I am the main heading (h1)

I am a sub-heading (h2)

I am a paragraph title (h3)

I am just a plain 'ol paragraph. (p)

The typography chosen for the website has a simple yet professional. it almost seemlessly blends the serif typeface of sub titles and paragraph headings with the san serif typeface of the main title and the paragraph text. For the Serif font I am using the default type face of Times New Roman. while the the main title has the Bariole-regular fontface. The paragraph text has the Nunito fontface. 

                        

.ds-primary-title{
    font-family: 'bariol-regular',
    sans-serif;
    font-size: 2.5em;
}

.ds-secondary-title{
    margin: 10px 0;
    font-size: 2em;
}

.ds-terciary-title{
    margin: 10px 0;
    font-size: 1.5em;
}

.ds-paragraph-text{
    margin: 10px 0;
    font-family: 'nunito',
    sans-serif;
}

Buttons:



The buttons are easily distinguishable from the rest of the page. I have given them a box shadow to lift them off the page. They also have a hover effect to let users know that they have been interacted with. The green color will hopefully encourage users to trust the action attached to the button. 

                        

.ds-btn-pr{
    background-color: #3a45fc;
    padding: 20px;
    border-radius: 5px;
    width: 30%;
    box-shadow: 0 5px 10px rgba(0,0,0, 0.5);
    font-size: 1em;
    font-family: 'nunito',sans-serif;
    transition: all .3s ease-in-out;
    margin-top: 10px;
    color: white;
    
}

.ds-btn-pr:hover{
        background-color:#33cc99;
        transform: scale(1.1);
        color: black;
}
